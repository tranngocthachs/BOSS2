New Password page

The New Password page allows you to request a new password in the case of forgetting your current one. The new password will be emailed to you.

Requesting a new password

To request a new password, fill in the form on the left column of the page. You will need to fill in the following details:

  • Your username, typically a student or staff number
  • Email address, typically your institution email address (e.g., j.bloggs@warwick.ac.uk)
  • A confirmation code to prevent automated spam: type the image exactly as you see it.

Click 'New password...' after filling in your details. Upon success, a new password will be emailed to your address.

Login troubleshooting

If you are confused, are not sure what your credentials are, or have problems with logging in, click the 'Login/Registration help' link under the right hand column.

Help

On the bottom right of the page there are three links:

  • Help, to take you to the online help.
  • About BOSS, to take you to the credits for the BOSS2 system.
  • Accessibility, to take you to a page describing the accessibility features of the BOSS2 system.
