Login page

The login page is displayed automatically when your credentials are required to access a page. It allows you to provide your credentials and continue to the destination page.

Logging in

To log in to BOSS2, type your username and password into the left hand column and click 'Log in...'. Upon a successful login you will be taken automatically to the section that you were navigating to. If you navigated to the login page manually, you will be taken to a generic 'logged in' page.

Forgotten credentials

If you have forgotten your password, click the 'Forgotten your password?' link next to the password box.

Login troubleshooting

If you are confused, are not sure what your credentials are, or have problems with logging in, click the 'Login/Registration help' link under the right hand column.

Help

On the bottom right of the page there are three links:

  • Help, to take you to the online help.
  • About BOSS, to take you to the credits for the BOSS2 system.
  • Accessibility, to take you to a page describing the accessibility features of the BOSS2 system.
