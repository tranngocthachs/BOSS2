All student pages

All student pages have the same basic layout, which is described below.

Title, breadcrumbs and greeting

Under the logo is the title of the current page. Below this is a set of 'breadcrumbs', showing the navigational hierachy of the current page. You may click any page in the navigational hiearchy to return to that page.

On the top right is a greeting showing your name and providing a 'Log out' link that will log out you out.

Student navigation menu

On the left hand of the page is a menu providing links to navigate the student section of the BOSS2 system. The items in the menu are:

  • 'Submit a solution', which will take you to a page listing your open assignments and providing the option to submit solutions for them.
  • 'Previous submissions', which will take you to a page listing assignments that you have provided submissions for and providing the option to review these submissions.
  • 'Show my account', which will take you to a page listing your details and providing you the option to change your password.
  • 'Log out', which will log you out of the BOSS2 system.

Help

On the bottom right of the page there are three links:

  • Help, to take you to the online help.
  • About BOSS, to take you to the credits for the BOSS2 system.
  • Accessibility, to take you to a page describing the accessibility features of the BOSS2 system.
