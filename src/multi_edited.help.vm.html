Operation Success page

The Operation Success Page is displayed if the database was updated successfully by your actions. You may proceed with your operations by clicking the 'Continue...' button.

Continuing

Click the 'Continue...' button to dismiss the notification of success and proceed.

Help

On the bottom right of the page there are three links:

  • Help, to take you to the online help.
  • About BOSS, to take you to the credits for the BOSS2 system.
  • Accessibility, to take you to a page describing the accessibility features of the BOSS2 system.
