Auto-assign markers page

The auto-assign markers page allows you to automatically generate marking assignments to bind markers to students.

Auto-assigning markers

To auto-assign markers, select the method by which you want to assign markers, and click the 'Perform Selected' button.

NOTE: The 'All markers to a student' method can create a very large amount of mappings (for example, 30 students and 4 markers would generate 120 mappings).
