All student pages

All student pages have the same basic layout, which is described here.

Student Details page

The Student Details page shows you information about your registration with the BOSS2 System, and allows you to change your password.

Personal Information

The details page contains a Personal Information table showing the information that the BOSS2 system holds about you. This information can only be updated by an administrator, and is as follows:

  • 'Student ID', your username and unique identifier to BOSS2. Usually your University ID number.
  • 'Name', the name that BOSS2 knows you by and will address you as.
  • 'E-mail address', the e-mail address that BOSS2 will use to communicate with you.

Changing your password

The details page also contains a password reset form. To reset your password, type the same password into both of the boxes of the password reset form and click the 'Change' button.
