Logout page

The logout page is displayed once you have logged out of the BOSS2 system. It provides links to different parts of the site.

Section Links

In the right hand column there are four links ('student', 'marker', 'staff' and 'admin') that will navigate you to the various sections of the site. These sections are:

  • Student section, for submitting solutions to assignments.
  • Marker section, for marking submitted solutions.
  • Staff section, for managing assignments.
  • Admin section, for managing people, modules and the database.

Clicking them will take you to those sections directly, after prompting you for login credentials.

Help

On the bottom right of the page there are three links:

  • Help, to take you to the online help.
  • About BOSS, to take you to the credits for the BOSS2 system.
  • Accessibility, to take you to a page describing the accessibility features of the BOSS2 system.
