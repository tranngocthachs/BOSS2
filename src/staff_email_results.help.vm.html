Email results page

The email results page allows you to mail results out to students in bulk.

Emailomg results

To email the results to students, select the students you wish to mail the results to, click confirmation checkbox and click the 'Perform' button.

If you do not wish to email the results, you can navigate away from the page using the breadcrumbs, menu, or browser back button.

NOTE: Students with incomplete results will not be checked by default.
